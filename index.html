scoreBoard

a simple tool for displaying the score of any game

Introduction

No matter whether you play soccer, football, basketball or just count score in integers scoreBoard can help you with that. But not only with that. If you hold an event where multiple players (or teams) compete against each other it can help you organize them in groups and also generate matches they should play against each other.

Once you use scoreBoard for keeping track of the score it can also generate live results and display them.

Give it a try on the demo page!
